Tony stark

Anthony Edward Stark, more commonly known as Tony Stark,
is a fictional character primarily portrayed by Robert Downey
Jr. in the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU) media franchise
—based on the Marvel Comics character of the same name
—commonly known by his alias, Iron Man. Stark is initially
depicted as an industrialist, genius inventor, and playboy who is
CEO of Stark Industries.
